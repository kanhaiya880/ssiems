Shri. Satish Chavan

Secretary

Since its inception in 1959, the MSP Mandal has exhibited relentless dedication in the pursuit of its dual objectives. The first goal involves bridging the gap in education accessibility by extending its reach to the underprivileged and deserving students, thereby ensuring that education transcends economic barriers. The second objective, equally paramount, revolves around delivering an unparalleled standard of education. Through unwavering commitment and effort, the MSP Mandal has managed to not only make education accessible to those at society's margins but has also elevated the caliber of education imparted. This remarkable journey stands as a testament to the organization's unwavering commitment to both inclusivity and excellence in education.
